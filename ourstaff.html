Meet Our Staff

Our dedicated team at The Nautical Mile is committed to providing you with an exceptional dining experience. Each member brings passion, expertise, and a love for fresh seafood and coastal cuisine.

Captain James Morrison — Executive Chef

With over 15 years of culinary experience, Chef James leads our kitchen with creativity and precision. His specialty is transforming fresh, local catches into unforgettable dishes that celebrate the flavors of the sea.

Marina Chen — Head Server

Marina ensures every guest feels welcome from the moment they walk through our doors. Her attention to detail and warm hospitality have made her a favorite among our regular patrons.

Thomas "Tommy" Blake — Sous Chef

Tommy's expertise in seafood preparation and his innovative approach to classic dishes help keep our menu exciting and delicious. He works closely with local fishermen to source the finest ingredients.

Sarah Williams — Restaurant Manager

Sarah oversees our daily operations with efficiency and grace. Her commitment to excellence ensures that every aspect of your visit runs smoothly, from reservations to your final course.
