Our Voyage

The Nautical Mile was founded with one simple idea: celebrate Ipswich’s maritime roots while offering a modern, welcoming dining experience by the water. From our anchor-inspired emblem to the sea-salted flavours on the plate, every detail nods to the town’s proud coastal story.

The Crew Behind the Mile

Our team — chefs, front of house, and crew — are all passionate about hospitality. We work with local suppliers and fishermen across Suffolk to bring honest, seasonal dishes to your table. Whether you’re celebrating or simply sharing a meal, expect relaxed service with thoughtful attention to detail.
